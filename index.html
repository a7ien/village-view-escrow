Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does an escrow company do?

As a neutral third party, we hold funds and documents in a secure account until all conditions of the real estate agreement are met, ensuring a safe transfer for both buyer and seller.

How long does a typical escrow take?

A standard residential escrow typically takes 30 to 45 days, though this can vary based on financing, inspections, and the specific terms of the contract.

Why choose an independent escrow company?

Independent firms are regulated by the Department of Financial Protection and Innovation (DFPI), which maintains the highest standards of oversight, financial liquidity, and bonding requirements. By not being affliated or influenced by any real estate brokerage, title company or lending institution we offer truly unbiased and independent services for our clients.

Can you handle transactions outside of the South Bay?

Yes, while we are rooted in the South Bay, we serve clients and handle transactions throughout all of Los Angeles and the entire state of California.
